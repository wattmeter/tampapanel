Advanced Capabilities & Quality Assurance

  • Industrial Joos German Hot Press: Held at an exact 120 °C (248 °F) under 5 bar (73 psi) pressure, our press delivers void-free bonds and consistent clamping across every panel—unmatched by vacuum bagging.
  • Humidity-Controlled Environment: We maintain 50 – 55 % RH with dual industrial dehumidifiers, preventing warping, seal-failure, and substrate decay for flawless lay-ups.
  • Thermal-Imaging Inspection: Every panel is infrared-scanned in real time to catch temperature variance, cold spots, or adhesive voids before they become defects.
  • Precision Stacking & Weighting: Custom rack and weight setups protect panel flatness during cure and storage, so your sheets leave our shop perfectly stable and ready to install.
